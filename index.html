🏆 World Cup 2026 Predictor

Unofficial fan-made prediction game — not affiliated with or endorsed by FIFA. All team names belong to their respective federations.

Click "Win/Draw" buttons or type exact scores for group matches. Click a team's name in the knockout bracket to pick it as the winner. Toggle "Official results" on a group or round once you've entered the real scores for it, rather than a guess. Everything is saved locally in your browser.
